Keyword research focuses on identifying queries people search for and mapping them to pages.
Steps:
1) Define your primary topics and audience.
2) Collect seed keywords from services/products and competitor pages.
3) Expand with variations: questions, comparisons, local modifiers, and problems.
4) Analyze search intent: informational, commercial, transactional, navigational.
5) Group keywords into clusters (topic + intent).
6) Prioritize by business value, difficulty, and topical coverage.
Deliverables:
- Keyword list with volume (optional), intent, priority, and target URL
- Content briefs for each cluster
