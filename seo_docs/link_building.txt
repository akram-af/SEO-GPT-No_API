Link building aims to earn editorial links that improve authority and discovery.
Safe methods:
- Linkable assets: original research, statistics, calculators, templates.
- Digital PR: pitch journalists with data or unique stories.
- Guest posting: only on relevant sites with real editorial quality.
- Resource page outreach: find curated lists and suggest your best resource.
- Broken link building: find dead links and offer your content as replacement.
Avoid:
- Buying links, PBNs, spam comments, irrelevant directories, automated outreach spam.
Quality signals:
- Relevance to your niche
- Real traffic and indexed pages
- Editorial placement within content
- Natural anchor text
